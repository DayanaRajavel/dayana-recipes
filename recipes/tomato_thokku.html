Tomota Thokku


Tomato Thokku is a flavorful Tamil-style condiment made with tomatoes, red chilies, and tamarind, slow-cooked with aromatic spices. It has a rich, tangy, and slightly spicy taste, enhanced by sesame oil and roasted fenugreek-mustard powder. This versatile thokku pairs perfectly with rice, dosa, idli, or chapati.

Ingredients

  • 15 tomatos
  • 8 to 10 red dry chilli
  • small piece tamarind
  • curry leaves
  • 10 to 20 garlic pieces
  • oil(Sesame oil - Recommended if you have) & mustard
  • fenugreek seeds
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Cook tomatoes, red chili, and tamarind until the water evaporates. Let it cool and blend coarsely (about 80% smooth).
  2. Dry roast fenugreek and mustard seeds, then grind them into a fine powder.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and garlic. Saut√©, then add the blended tomato mix and salt. Cook until oil separates, then mix in the spice powder.

Now Tomato thokku is ready! :)
