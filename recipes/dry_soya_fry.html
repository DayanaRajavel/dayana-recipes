Dry Soya Fry (or Chicken Fry)

This flavorful Dry Soya Fry (or Chicken Fry) is a delicious, easy-to-make dish with a rich blend of spices and a roasted coconut-based paste. Perfect as a side or a main dish, this recipe is sure to become a family favorite. The combination of aromatic spices, ginger-garlic paste, and a coconut paste base creates a savory dish that can be enjoyed with rice or flatbreads.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt & Fresh coriander leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 cup soya chunks (or chicken)
  • tomato paste

For the Paste:

  • 2 tbsp coconut slices
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • A few curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Roast coconut slices, onion, fennel seeds, and curry leaves until golden. Blend into a paste.
  2. Heat oil, sauté sliced onion until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for a minute, then add tomato paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add soya chunks (or chicken), cook on low heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the prepared paste, stir, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish with green chilies and coriander leaves. Cook until the dish is dry and well-cooked. Serve hot.

That’s it—enjoy this recipe with rice :)
